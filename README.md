# The-Bite-of-Two-22
The year 2026 hasn't been easy for the professors at TMU. A series of mysterious murders have begun occurring around campus, and students worry as their friends begin disappearing left and right. Take the role of one of three intrepid (and whimsical) students, as they attempt to crack the case... or die trying.
